(Loan Characteristics & Borrower's APR)¶

by (Hatim Alsulami)¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of loans that could be used to predict the borrower APR. The main focus was on the original loan amount, borrower's Prosper rating, loan term, Employment status, borrower's stated monthly income and monthly loan's payment.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others. After wrangling process we ended up with 84853 loans. We are only intrested on loans originatedfrom July 2009.

(Distribution of Borrower APR)¶

The distribution of APR looks multimodal. A small peak centered around 0.1, a large peak around at 0.2. There is also a small peak centered 0.3. Additionally, there is a very shape peak between 0.35 and 0.36
