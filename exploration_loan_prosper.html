What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 84853 loans in the dataset with 9 features. Most variables are numirc in nature. EmploymentStatus is string type.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I'm most interested in figuring out what features are best for predicting the borrower's Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for the loan.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I expect that the LoanOriginalAmount will have the strongest effect APR : the larger the loan, the lower the APR. I also think that the Term, ProsperRating, EmploymentStatus, StatedMonthlyIncome and MonthlyLoanPayment will have effects on the APR, though to a much smaller degree than the main effect of LoanOriginalAmount .
